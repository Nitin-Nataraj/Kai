COVID-19 Safety Measures


One of our highest priorities is the health, safety, and security of our guests, team members, and business partners.

COVID-19 has fundamentally changed the way we live, and we are adjusting our daily operations to ensure a safe and secure stay at Kai International. We and a team of experts have reviewed our existing health and safety processes and developed a new safety protocol.

  • A special task force sanitizes every part of the hotel every 3 hours.
  • All the hotel staff are completely vaccinated and take an RT-PCR test everyday.
  • All items coming in or going out of the hotel is thoroughly sanitized.
  • We grow our own vegetables organically to ensure the safest possible experience.
  • Any meat being served is procured only after oassing several safety checks to avoid possible contamination.
