War Against COVID!!!

Kai International becomes one of the first international hotel chains with completely sanitized campuses and fully vaccinated working staff. Customer Safety is our number one priority, says Founder and Chairman Patrick Specter.

Choice of Champions

Party Mode ON!!!! - After a thrilling victory in the finale in 2018, the members of the Golden State Warriors celebrated the end of their successful NBA season with yet another trophy at The Kai International LA.

Expansion

The expansion plans for new hotels at one of the prime holiday spots - Mauritius and the tech hub of India - Bangalore, are currently under discussion. New Hotels to be expcected by early 2025, says CEO Sean Duncan.

The Charity Event

Indian Business Tycoon Mukesh Ambani conducts a charity get-together at Kai International Mumbai. Several Indian celebrities like Amitabh Bachchan, AR Rahman and Alia Bhatt glorified the event with their presence.

Snippets from Kai

CEO Sean Duncan encourages customers to get completely vaccinated before entering the hotels. Customers are required to produce their vaccination certificate while booking rooms and before entering the hotels. Strict safety measures should be followed inside the Hotel campus. The rooms will be frequently cleaned. Swimming pools and similar facilities that might attract a huge crowd will be accesible for a restricted period of the day.

The university scholarship initiative from Kai International has produced astonishing results. Many students from africa and south-east asia have benefitted from the program, says the director general of UNESCO Audrey Azoulay. After meetings with the Co-founder and President Jagadish Jeevanandham and the Aftica Head, Mr. Uhwewe Smith, a partnership was formed between the two entities and this hints towards the creation of more univeristy scholarships for talended students in future.
