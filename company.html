The Team

As of November 21, 2021

Sean Duncan

Chief Executive Officer

Sean Duncan is the CEO of Kai International and an alumnus of the prestigious Wharton School of Business. Duncan has extensive global executive leadership experience in the hospitality, leisure, and FMCG sectors. Prior to joining Kai, he was the Director of Operations at the QE Hotel Group, where he led a major turnaround of the company and also oversaw their European expansion. He joined Kai in 2017 and worked on the Asian and Australian Expansions.



Amelia Peters

Americas Head

Mrs. Peters is the current Americas Head. She is responsible for about 1/4th of all our hotels. She has a B.A. in Hospitality Business and an M.Sc in Marketing Research from the Michigan State University. She worked along with Melissa Pearson to expand the presence of the hotel chain in America (North and South).



Avinash Singhania

Asia Head

Mr. Singhania is the current Asia Head. He holds a B.Tech. in Metallurgical Engineering from IIT Bombay and an MBA-PGPX (Post-Graduate Programme in Management for Executives) from IIM Ahmedabad. She worked along with Melissa Pearson to expand the presence of the hotel chain in Europe.



Nora Beatrix

Europe Head

Ms. Beatrix is the current Europe Head. She holds a B.Sc in Technische Bestuurskunde and an M.Sc in Strategic Product Design from TU Delft. She worked along with Jane Sanders to expand the presence of the hotel chain in Europe.



Noah Warner

Australia Head

Mr. Warner is the current Australia Head. He holds a Undergraduate degree in Industrial Design and a Masters Degree in Human Resource Management from the University of Canberra. He worked along with David Scott to expand the presence of the hotel chain in Australia.



Uhwewe Smith

Africa Head

Mr. Smith is the current Africa Head. He holds a B.Sc in Environmental and Geographical Science and an Executive MBA from the University of Cape Town. He worked along with Rachel Agwuegbo to expand the presence of the hotel chain in Africa.
