Who / What is a Kaizen?

Kaizen is a rewards/membership programme. Alternatively, it refers to a guest who owns the membership. This programme is an effort to aid in providing the best experience for a guest at our chain of hotels. A blend of the words, "Kai" and "Citizen", concisely explains the philosophy behind the name.


Why become a Kaizen?

The personalized membership cards make you and your travel partners preferred guests at the hotel. Enjoy your special moments through the membership year with specially curated offers and celebrations.


The Kaizen Advantage

Complete access to all our facilities and adventure activities that would otherwise incur charges (most activities are free as is, but some of them are premium). Complimentary Breakfast. Even a free and all expense paid holiday*, what more could you ask for?
