Embracing Carbon Neutrality:
We strictly adhere to a carbon free campus and we also embrace our workers and staffs in all our hotels to follow the same. All our campuses are designed with a sustainable landscape and a sustainable water management system, with a high dependence on renewable energy sources. Currently, we aim on leaving a very minute carbon footprint and in the long run, we expect to reach the carbon neutrality stage, hence becoming a 100% Carbon neutral and Sustainable Campus.

Univerity Scholarships:
Every year, we share our profits with talented young minds from 3rd world countries who cannot afford to pursue their higher studies because of financial barriers. We offer university scholarships to more than 2000 students every year at various universities across the globe.

Free Meals Initiative:
Poverty and Hunger issues keep growing day by day and with the ongoing pandemic, the above problems are at an all time high. We try our best to fight against these problems, through our free meals initiative in compliance with UN's initiative to solve poverty and world hunger. We offer free meals to poverty stricken countries as a measure towards improving the livelihoods of the people in those countries.
